"The Side Step"

By Mikkel Lamb | Posted on: 06/22/2020

The side step is such an amazing move because it gives you the advanget of creating extra space form you defender before taking a shot. Adding this to your game will take your game to the next level of competition. This move is considered advanced. The reason its advanced is because of the mechanics. The requirements for this move are timing and complete balance. Without balance most jump shots out of that move would be diffccult to make.
